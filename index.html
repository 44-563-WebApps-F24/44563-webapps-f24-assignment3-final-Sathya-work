Hyderabad

Hyderabad is my favorite city because it mixes old and new in a special way. The city has a rich history with beautiful places like the Charminar and Golconda Fort. At the same time, it is growing fast with new businesses and technology. The food, especially the biryani, is delicious and famous. I like how the city feels both modern and traditional, making it a great place to live and enjoy.

Places to visit in Hyderabad

  1. Charminar
  2. Golconda Fort
  3. Hussain Sagar
  • Nagarjuna Sagar
  • Ramoji Film City
  • Ananthagiri Hills
    • Beautiful hiking trails
    • Calm and peaceful environment
